About MapIT Design (MID)

MapIT Design (MID) is a specialized solar design and engineering support partner helping solar installers, EPC companies, and renewable energy contractors across the United States and Canada deliver faster, AHJ-compliant solar projects.

We provide end-to-end solar design solutions, including Aurora Solar proposals, AHJ-compliant permit plan sets, electrical and structural documentation, PE-stamped drawings, and grid interconnection support.

Our experienced design team understands the challenges solar companies face when project volume increases from tight deadlines and AHJ revisions to complex utility requirements and structural analysis.

With expertise in NEC, CEC, AHJ requirements, utility standards, and solar industry best practices, we support residential, commercial, and battery storage projects with reliability and precision.

From initial customer proposal to final approval, MapIT Design helps solar companies build faster, smarter, and more efficiently.

Our End-to-End Workflow

We support every stage of the solar design process:

  1. Aurora Proposal Design
    Create professional solar proposals with optimized system layouts, production estimates, and financial analysis to help your sales team close more projects.
  2. Site & Roof Assessment
    Review site information, roof geometry, utility requirements, satellite imagery, and customer documentation to ensure every project starts with accurate design data.
  3. Permit Plan Sets
    Prepare complete AHJ-compliant permit packages, including site plans, roof layouts, single-line diagrams, three-line diagrams, attachment layouts, equipment specifications, and NEC & local code compliance notes.
  4. PE Stamping Support
    Coordinate Professional Engineer (PE) reviews and stamping where required by local jurisdictions.
  5. Permitting Support
    Assist with permit revisions, correction comments, and documentation updates to help speed up approval.
  6. Utility Interconnection
    Prepare utility application packages and required documentation for interconnection approval.
  7. Permission to Operate (PTO)
    Provide support through the final approval process until your customer receives Permission to Operate.
